Presentation framing

The story to tell the manager

Little Kitchen already has the history, the menu depth, and the neighborhood reputation. The opportunity is to add a polished digital front-of-house layer that feels like a smart host, not a generic chatbot, while keeping the restaurant’s Westport warmth and established identity front and center.

Positioning

“Since 1987, Little Kitchen has been a local favorite for Chinese, Malaysian, Thai, Indonesian, Taiwanese, sushi, takeout, delivery, and bar service. This demo shows how the next version of the website can answer like a maître d’, convert more guests directly, and reduce interruption at the host stand.”

Presentation goal

Help the manager see this as a practical hospitality tool: better service, faster guest response, more direct revenue, and a cleaner staff workflow during busy periods.
